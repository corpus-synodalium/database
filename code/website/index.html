Local Ecclesiastical Legislation


This is a database-in-progress of extant local ecclesiastical legislation issued across Latin Christendom between 1215-1400. The corpus includes mainly diocesan statutes and provincial canons, but other scattered genres appear on occasion. Currently, the database includes more than 900 texts, with roughly 400 still to be added. Some texts have been transcribed directly from manuscripts, but most are taken from modern and early modern editions.

Generous financial support for this project has been provided by the Milton Fund at Harvard University; Stanford's School of Humanities & Sciences, and the Center for Spatial and Textual Analysis; and a grant from the FACE Foundation's Thomas Jefferson Fund.

For further details, please contact Rowan Dorin (dorin@stanford.edu).
